3D Transformations: SE(3)¶

The group of all transformations in the 3D Cartesian space is . Transformations consist of a rotation and a translation. Those can be represented in multiple different ways.

One of the most convenient ways to represent transformations are transformation matrices. A transformation matrix is a 4x4 matrix of the form

It is a partitioned matrix with a 3x3 rotation matrix and a column vector that represents the translation. It is also sometimes called the homogeneous representation of a transformation.
